FAQ
Why isn't GPS working?▼
Grant location access when prompted. On iOS: Settings → Safari → Location → Allow. On Android: check Chrome's app permissions. GPS, compass and altitude all require this permission.
How do offline maps work?▼
Map tiles are cached automatically as you browse. For full offline sectors, go to Maps, tap "📦 Offline", resize the box to your desired area and tap "DOWNLOAD SECTOR". The Service Worker caches all tiles in that box.
What is the Atomic Chronometer?▼
ION fetches time from WorldTimeAPI (NTP-backed) on launch to synchronize its clock to within ±5ms. The display updates at 60fps for millisecond precision.
Can ION be used completely offline?▼
Yes. GPS, compass, and the atomic clock all work offline. Pre-cached map tiles, last weather data, and saved routes stay accessible. An "OFFLINE" badge appears when cached data is being shown.
How does the transit search work?▼
ION displays routes from fused 9292, Google Transit, and GTFS data. Enter your destination and ION suggests buses, trains, and walking routes with live delays and pricing.
Who built ION?▼
ION is built by CuraHack — the Hack Club chapter for Curaçao developers. Visit curahack.club to learn more and join.
